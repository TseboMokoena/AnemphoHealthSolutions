About Us

Anempho Health Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female-owned company established in 2019. The company is run by a multi-skilled professional nurse that aims to provide expeditious, convenient, efficient, and comprehensive health care services in accordance with the Occupational Health and Safety Act and all applicable employment legislation.

We affiliated and registered with the following bodies:

  • SANC - South African Nursing Council
  • BHFSA - Board of Health Care Funders Of South Africa
  • SASOHN - South African Society Of Occupational Health Nursing Practioners
